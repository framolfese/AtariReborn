"Retro-marcia"

November 13, 2018 by M&M

A quick step back on how and why we chose to talk about Atari. Ideas, discussions, memories and more.

We wanted a project that could be an innovative idea and at the same time could approach any kind of user. Discussing on how to accomplish this, we came to the conclusion that surely one of the things that are most dear to people are their memories. But memories can come out thanks to any kind of topic or emotion.

Therefore, having in front of us a vast field of choices, we asked ourselves: how can we combine the beauty of memory with ours passions? And from here, knowing that in addition to information technology our most present passion is certainly the videogame, we decided to find a compromise to this triad of elements.

And so Atari Reborn was born, a project that easily guides the user on a journey through his memories to rediscover the beauty of retro-gaming.
