PACMAN

Pac-Man is a video game designed by Toru Iwatani and produced by Namco in 1980 in the arcade format. In the West it was licensed by Midway Games. Immediately acquired great popularity and, in the following years, under the label Namco have been published several versions for almost all consoles and computers, preserving until today its reputation as a classic video games.

DONKEY KONG

Donkey Kong is an arcade video game platform developed by Nintendo and released in arcades in 1981. It was later converted for numerous types of computers and consoles, as well as Game & Watch. In the game appear for the first time the famous characters Mario (to know more about the role of Atari in the choice of the name, click on the button History at the top) and Donkey Kong.
